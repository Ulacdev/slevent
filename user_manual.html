StartupLab User Manual

Official Role Pages & Functions | Version 1.0

This document explains what each page in the system does for each user role.

1. ADMIN PAGES

These pages are for overall platform management and control.

Admin Dashboard Complete overview of all events, total site earnings, and recent platform activity.
Events Management A central list where admins can oversee and moderate every event on the system.
Users Management Manage all user accounts including Organizers, Attendees, and Staff members.
Subscription Plans Manage and create the pricing plans that organizers pay for to use the platform.
Support Tickets Handle bug reports and technical help requests from all users.
Payment Settings Configure the platform’s payout methods and global commission settings.
Email Templates Manage the automatic emails that get sent to users across the platform.
Audit Logs History for security purposes to see who did what on the system.

2. ORGANIZER PAGES

Pages for event creators to manage their events and business.

Welcome Home The landing page after login with quick actions to start creating your first event.
Organizer Dashboard Real-time statistics for your events including daily sales and revenue charts.
My Events Your personal list of events where you can edit details or see check-in status.
Create Event The main page where you input event names, dates, locations, and descriptions.
Attendees List View and manage the directory of everyone who has registered for your events.
Check-In Scanner A mobile-ready tool to scan guest QR codes for fast entry at the venue.
Sales Reports Downloadable data and detailed reports about your event performance.
Subscription Plans Choose or upgrade your plan to increase your attendee and feature limits.
Team & Access Invite helpers (Staff) to your team and set what tasks they are allowed to do.
Organization Info Set your organization's logo and public name for your events.
Organizer Support Dedicated channel for Organizers to contact platform admins.
Account Settings Change your password and other personal login details.

3. STAFF PAGES

Simplified dashboard for assistants at the event location.

My Events See the list of events where you are helping with check-ins.
Guest List Search for guests by name or email if they do not have their ticket ready.
Scanner Tool Scan attendee QR codes to mark them as "checked-in" instantly.

4. ATTENDEE PAGES

Pages used by the public to find events and get tickets.

Event Discovery The search page for finding events by category or location.
Event Details Read about the event, its date, and view the different ticket prices.
Registration Buy your ticket by filling in your information and checking out.
My Tickets A personal dashboard where you can see all your active and past tickets.
Ticket View The page showing your unique QR code for entry.
Liked Events The events you have saved for later.
