## Introduction
The **StartupLab Business Ticketing System** is an enterprise-grade event management platform designed for organizers to create, sell, and manage events with ease. 
The platform provides a seamless experience for:
*   **Attendees**: Discover events, register securely, and manage digital tickets.
*   **Organizers**: Professional tools for event lifecycle management, real-time sales intelligence, and attendee coordination.
*   **Staff**: Specialized venue entry tools for high-speed QR validation and attendee assistance.
*   **Administrators**: Centralized control over platform-wide settings, subscription infrastructure, and system audits.

---

## System Access
Access the system via any modern desktop or mobile web browser at:
**[https://startuplab-event-creation.vercel.app/#/](https://startuplab-event-creation.vercel.app/#/)**

*   **Landing Page**: Browse public events, filter by category or location.
*   **Organizer Login**: Sign in or create an account to start hosting events.

## 🛡️ PART A — GUEST & ATTENDEE POV

### 1. Login & Registration

#### 1.1 Signing In
Users sign in using their registered email address and password, or through social providers.

**Steps to sign in:**
1.  Go to the system URL.
2.  Click **Sign In** on the top navigation bar.
3.  Enter your **Email** and **Password**, or click **Sign in with Google**.
4.  Accept **Terms and Conditions** if prompted.
5.  Click **Sign In**.

| Field / Element | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Email** | Your registered account email. |
| **Password** | Your secure account password. |
| **Forgot Password?** | Trigger a password reset link to your registered email. |
| **Create Account** | Link for new users to register a new account. |

#### 1.2 Creating an Account
New users can register to save tickets, follow organizers, and access personalized event feeds.

| Field | Required | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Full Name** | Yes | Your public display name. |
| **Email** | Yes | Verification and login address. |
| **Password** | Yes | Minimum 8 characters recommended. |
| **Confirm Password** | Yes | Must match the above. |
| **Terms & Conditions** | Yes | Required for privacy and compliance. |

## Security & Compliance
*   **Session Security**: For your protection, sessions are automatically expired after **7 minutes of inactivity** (as of April 2026).
*   **Verification**: All new accounts require email verification before they can host events or purchase high-value tickets.
*   **Privacy**: We adhere to strict data privacy standards; your event data and attendee lists are never shared outside your organization.
