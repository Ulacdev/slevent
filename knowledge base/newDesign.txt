PROMPT START

You are redesigning an existing project to strictly align with the StartupLab Business Center Brand Guidelines (v2.0).

You MUST follow these rules exactly.
Any deviation is NOT allowed.

1. Brand Authority (Non-Negotiable)

The StartupLab Brand Guidelines v2.0 are the single source of truth.
If any existing design, layout, color, font, or component conflicts with the brand guidelines, it MUST be changed to comply with the brand.

Do NOT introduce new visual styles, colors, fonts, or logo variations.

2. Logo Rules (STRICT)

Use only approved StartupLab logos

Select logo versions based on background contrast (light vs dark)

NEVER:

Stretch, rotate, skew, or distort the logo

Change logo colors

Add shadows, gradients, outlines, or effects

Recreate or redraw the logo

Logo must always be clear, readable, and high-contrast

If the logo placement violates these rules, it MUST be fixed.

3. Color Palette (STRICT)

Only the following colors are allowed:

#003E86

#3768A2

#38BDF2

#F2F2F2

#2E2E2F

Rules:

Do NOT introduce any new colors

No gradients unless explicitly shown in the brand guide (none allowed by default)

Use colors consistently across all screens and components

Prioritize clarity, contrast, and professionalism

4. Typography (STRICT)

Allowed fonts ONLY:

Helvetica

Neue / Neue Helvetica

Rules:

No decorative, display, or alternative fonts

Maintain clean, professional hierarchy

If existing typography does not match, replace it

5. Iconography

Icons must be clean, minimal, and professional

Must visually align with the StartupLab brand tone

No playful, hand-drawn, overly detailed, or inconsistent icon styles

6. Design Tone & Feel

The final design MUST communicate:

Professionalism

Trust

Stability

Safety

Business credibility

Avoid:

Trendy or experimental UI styles

Over-decoration

Excessive animation or effects

7. Redesign Behavior

You are redesigning, not inventing

Preserve functionality and content

Change visuals ONLY when required to meet brand compliance

If unsure, choose the most conservative, brand-safe option

8. Validation Requirement

Before finalizing:

Verify that every screen complies with:

Logo rules

Color palette

Typography

Brand tone

If any element is non-compliant, revise it

Compliance is more important than creativity.
